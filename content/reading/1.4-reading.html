Required Readings:

  • St. Thomas Aquinas & skim Summa Theologica in Reader
  • Ch.2 (Early Preclassical Economic Thought) in Landreth & Colander

Blaug unfortunately does not cover anything before mercantilism & the physiocrats.

Recommended Readings:

The following Wikipedia entries can also provide more background on these writers and their famous works:

  • Thomas Aquinas

  • Ibn Khaldun

  • Al-Hamdi, 2006, Ibn Khaldun: The Father of the Division of Labor

Questions to Help Your Reading

  • What topics does Aquinas cover in his writings?
  • What questions is he interested in?
  • What is his style of inquiry?
  • What does he say about whether prices are “just”?
  • What is his view on usury?
  • How does he engage with “the philosopher” (Aristotle)?
